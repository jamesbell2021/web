James Bell

Lecturer in Games Development at Belfast Metropolitan College, Castlereagh Campus, and Course Coordinator for the Level 3 Games Development programme.

Role & Responsibilities

I design and deliver an industry‑focused curriculum that prepares learners for progression into higher education or entry‑level roles within the games and creative technology industries.

As Course Coordinator, I oversee curriculum planning, assessment, quality assurance, and the integration of professional game‑development workflows aligned with studio practice.

Level 3 Games Development – Course Overview

The games industry is one of the fastest‑growing creative sectors globally. At Belfast Met, students choose a specialist pathway aligned to their strengths and ambitions.

Castlereagh Campus – Game Development

  • Unreal Engine and Blueprint scripting
  • Technical gameplay systems and mechanics
  • 2D, 3D, VR, and AR development
  • Game audio creation and implementation
  • Professional, project‑based studio workflows

Titanic Quarter Campus – Game Art & Design

  • Concept art and visual development
  • 2D & 3D modelling and animation
  • UI / UX and environment design
  • Portfolio‑focused creative practice

Teaching Philosophy

My approach is grounded in learning through making. Students learn by building, testing, failing, and refining ideas — mirroring professional development practice.
